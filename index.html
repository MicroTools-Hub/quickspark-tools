    Comprehensive Text Processing Tools

    Text manipulation forms the foundation of most digital work. Our suite includes twelve specialized text tools that cover every common scenario. The Word Counter provides comprehensive statistics including word count, character count, reading time, speaking time, readability scores, and keyword extraction—perfect for writers meeting word requirements, content marketers optimizing SEO, or students tracking assignment lengths.

    The Character Counter goes beyond basic counting, displaying total characters, characters without spaces, and UTF-8 byte counts, helping social media managers stay within platform limits and developers verify encoding requirements. Our Case Converter transforms text between uppercase, lowercase, title case, and sentence case instantly, saving minutes on repetitive formatting tasks that multiply across large documents.

    The Reverse Text tool offers character reversal, word reversal, and line reversal—useful for creative projects, palindrome checking, and specialized text effects. Remove Line Breaks eliminates unwanted spacing from copied content, a common frustration when transferring text between applications. The Lorem Ipsum generator creates professional placeholder text in customizable quantities for design mockups and wireframes.

    Find & Replace provides powerful search functionality with case-sensitive options and batch replacement capabilities, dramatically faster than manual find-and-replace in large documents. Sort Lines organizes text alphabetically or by length, removes duplicates, and reverses order—ideal for cleaning up lists, organizing data, and preparing content for processing.

    Count Specific Words tracks exact word frequency with case-sensitive or insensitive options, helping writers avoid repetition and optimize keyword density. Text Diff compares two versions side-by-side with visual highlighting of additions, deletions, and changes—essential for editors, developers reviewing code changes, and anyone managing document versions. Text to Speech converts written content into natural audio with adjustable voice, rate, and pitch options, supporting accessibility and multitasking.
